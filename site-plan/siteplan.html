Real Base Academy

Site Name

Real Base Academy is a modern learning platform designed to empower students with real-world skills. The name reflects a strong foundational approach to education and personal development.

Optional domain availability: realbaseacademy.edu

Site Purpose

The site will provide only basic information for about the school; what they offer, facilities, news blog and a contact section for visitors to reach the institution for further enquiries.

Scenarios

  • What is this institution all about?
  • What facilities do they have?
  • Do they have other campuses closer to me?

Color Schema

  • Blue (#002F6C): Used for headings, navigation, and borders.
  • Red (#C8102E): Used for call-to-action buttons, icons, and accents.

Typography

  • Roboto (Sans-serif): Used for body text for readability.
  • Merriweather (Serif): Used for headings to create a classic academic feel.
